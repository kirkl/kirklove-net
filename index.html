Panna

A short while ago I backed Panna on Kickstarter. It looked great and I loved the chefs involved, especially Jonathan Waxman from Barbuto here in NYC.

The app is now out to the general public and I have to say, it's really, really well done. You can download it here. It's ideal for people who like delcious, simple, easy-to-follow recipes.

While the app is free you will have to pay a subscription to unlock the entire upcoming year of recipes (my subscription was covered by my Kickstarter donation!). Thanks again to the folks behind Panna and to Kickstarter for making this happen.


The Panna App for iPad is flat out gorgeous and wonderfully done.
I highly recoommend it.
