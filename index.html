Dinner At Perla

Last night we went back to Perla. This time with really good friends. It was their first time there and that made it even more special. As usual it was amazing, from start to finish. My only regret is I'm under the weather and lost my voice half way though. Even with that, I loved every second. Psst: The new tajarin pasta dish is incredible.
