My Top 10 Indispensable Mac Tools

I've got a handful of tools I use that I just can't live without. I thought I would share them. If you are on Mac and want some quick, lightweight, but killer apps, here's your list:

  1. Alfred App: Hands down my workhorse. Use it for everything
  2. CloudApp: Perfect for quick screen caps and sharing
  3. Size Up: Can't live without this. Window management made perfect.
  4. Dropbox: They've out-Appled Apple by a mile.
  5. Sip: Slick little color picker. Invaluable
  6. Pendule: Great Chrome Extension for CSS & Web Development
  7. Sublime Text 2: My IDE of choice
  8. Wunderlist: Great note and ToDo managment
  9. YNAB: Slick budget/finance tool
  10. iTerm: Better alternative to the default Terminal App

I'll didn't include Sparrow (mailapp) in my list, because it's so ingrained in my workflow now (on mobile, too) that I don't consider it an add-on, but rather part of my system.

Would love to hear any suggestions from folks who have things they love. Always looking for cool toys to play with and boost productivity.
