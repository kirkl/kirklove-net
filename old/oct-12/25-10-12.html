My 2012 Albums Of The Year So Far

Last year I kept an album of the year list and it was tough getting it down to just ten. I'm doing the same for 2012 though this year not as many "albums" are grabbing me. There have been tons of great singles and one-offs as you can see on my exfm profile page, just not albums.

That said, the Grizzly Bear, Sea Wolf, and Cold Fronts albums are indeed fantastic and will be battling it out for the top three spots. Don't see any albums knocking them out. We'll see. The last quarter always seems to roll out some real gems.
