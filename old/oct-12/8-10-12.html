Family

There is nothing more important to me in this world than family and friends. They will always come first.

This past Saturday we celebrated my daughter's first birthday. Surrounded by family and friends I was struck by how blessed I am. Really, I'm beyond lucky. An incredible wife. Beautiful daughter. Two amazing parents. Two older brothers. And an amazing older sister who makes me laugh so hard. Plus, so many friends and family from around the globe. It was humbling and left me feeling beyond grateful.

There are days I forget how lucky I am. Saturday reminded me.
