Hunkered Down

We're stocked up and waiting for #Sandy aka "the storm". Right now it doesn't feel like it's going to come and will merely be a repeat of Irene. Hope we're right.

It's always weird when the city is so deserted. It's eerie and far too quiet. And a city, especially a city like Manhattan just doesn't feel right when it's quiet. I never thought I would get used to all the city sounds, but I guess I have and I miss them.

Working from home is definitely nice though. It's great to be able to walk to the other room and play with the Bean for a bit and hug Paraguay.

Stay safe everyone.
