Ethan Daniel Davidson

It's super rare for a song or artist to blow me away the first time I hear it. Ethan Daniel Davidson did. Just something about him hit me deep in my soul. Went on to listen to his entire album and this kid is just flat out special. It's got influences from George Harrison, The Beatles, Tom Waits, Neil Young, Echo and The Bunnymen, Spiritualized, and more. Never ceases to amaze me the sheer amount of incredible music being made.
