Ural Solo sT

I miss my old Ural Raven. She was a lot of work to keep running, but she was also a blast. Lately I find myself craving a Ural again, but sans the sidecar. Sidecars are fun, though riding solo has a meditative quality about it that I miss. I've done some of my best thinking strapped to the back of a bike speeding down a long country road.

Having a no garage and living in Manhattan a cycle is just too much work and headache. Someday though maybe I'll have a house again and if I'll do, I'll have this puppy in the garage to tinker on.
