Loss

If you've lost someone you know. One day they are here, the next they aren't and there isn't a damn thing you can do about it. It tears you up inside and makes you question everything.

It's been a while since I lost someone, though this date makes me think of it. A lot has changed since then. A lot. It may be clich√© to say, "Time heals all wounds", yet it's absolutely true, it does. Granted it takes a tremendous amount of effort and courage and determination as well, but mostly it just takes time.

Most folks involved in my life now don't even know about my past and I don't tell them. It's not important. What is important is that I'm still here and still living a great, full, happy life.
