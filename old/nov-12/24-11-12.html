ASU –> PTY –> JFK

We left Paraguay at 2 in the morning last night. It's now 4 PM and I'm writing this post from my home in New York City. The fact that you can travel nearly over 5,000 miles while sleeping, eating, or watching a movie never ceases to amaze me. I'm sure in less than a hundred years people will look back and laugh at us. But, I'm pretty damn impressed by that.

Oh yeah, the trip was awesome. More on that later.
