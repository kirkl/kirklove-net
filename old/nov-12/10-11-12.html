Friday Night Pizza Night

Every Friday we have Friday Night Pizza Night. It's arguably my favorite time of the week. It's simple and fun and chill and much needed after a stressful week.

Sometimes we make the pizza from scratch (something we should do more) and others we order or walk somewhere to get a pizza. Typically we order from Freddie & Peppers on 74th and Amsterdam. Quality pies from a crazy Latin crew. Sacco's (Sidenote: Why are all pizza place websites so atrocious?) between 54th and 55th on 9 Ave probably has the best pie, but sadly they don't deliver and it's a bit of a haul.

Other Friday's we have guests over and that's great, too. Lately though it's been low key with just myself, Paraguay and Bean. Then after Bean goes down Paraguay and I play a few hands of Mexe Mexe, a super fun Brazilian card game. Lili typically wins, but I hold my own.

The thing that matters is the ritual. It's something I look forward to each week and I've really come to love Friday Night Pizza Night.
