My Own Little World

"When you make a business, you’re making a little world where you control the laws. It doesn’t matter how things are done everywhere else. In your little world, you can make it like it should be."

– Derek Sivers

Over a year ago I bookmarked this quote from Derek Sivers. Every few weeks I'd go back and read it – a little world where you control the laws. It fascinated me and called to me. It would repeat, "Do it." Problem was I didn't know what kind of "world" I wanted to build. Then about six months ago I figured it out. At least in my head. And thus began the inevitability of today.

Today is my last day at ex.fm.

I suppose I could have taken the "safer" route and worked on my idea on nights and weekends. But, I didn't think that was fair to exfm as my heart and mind were being pulled toward "my little world". It also wasn't fair to me or my family.

I'm leaving exfm on the best of terms. I truly love the platform and believe 100% it's the absolute best way to discover music online. I'm incredibly grateful to have worked with a fantastic team. Dan, Marshall, and crew are doing a killer job. I will miss them, but they will move forward just fine. And I will move forward as well. (As my friend Maura likes to say, "Keep moving".)

All of this is incredibly exciting, liberating, scary, and unknown. I like ALL that. Details to follow. For now I'm off to Paraguay for a bit then full-speed ahead.

If you want a sneak peak or have questions feel free to write. I also want to take a minute and thank my amazing wife for believing in me and agreeing to take this chance alongside me. It's humbling. Sos la mejor mi vida. I also hope I can count on the support of my friends. I'll need that, too. Ok then, onward!
