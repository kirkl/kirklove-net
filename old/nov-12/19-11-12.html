Borí Borí

The Paraguayan Classic - Borí Borí

Just had the most amazing bowl of Borí Borí. It's like chicken soup, but trust me it ain't your grandmother's chicken soup. This soup is thick, heavy, and super delicious. It's also served with Mbeyú another classic Paraguayan dish which is starch, corn flour, eggs, pork fat, cheese and milk. Again, it ain't light, but it's incredible. A bowl of borí borí and a piece of mbeyú will fill you up like nothing us. It's so good.

Have I mentioned how much I love Paraguay?
