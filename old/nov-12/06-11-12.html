6 November 2012

Election Day 2012

Your vote matters. Please take the time to vote today. Personally, I've made no secret I'm voting for Obama. Deep down I still believe he's a good man who wants to do the right thing. Unfortunately, he made the crucial mistake of over-promising and under-delivering. Though he has done a lot and I respect him for trying. It's a tough gig and the wheels of government turn excruciatingly slow.

I have a feeling Obama will win rather easily and when presented with a second term where he doesn't have to "worry" about getting re-elected he will shine. He will build on his experience of the first four years and we'll see the great President we had all thought we were getting four years ago. That's my HOPE.
