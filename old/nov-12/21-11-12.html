La Mision

This afternoon and tonight Lili and I will be staying at La Mision. I really love it there. It's a beautiful hotel. Easily the nicest in all of Paraguay. The staff is super friendly, they have great food, wonderful spacious rooms and a great pool on the roof. We're calling it our one-day second honeymoon since the Bean isn't coming (she's staying with Lili's sister Estella). Family is great, but sometimes you need a nice relaxing day alone.
