Content

For the longest time I always believed you needed to be "hungry" if you wanted to be successful and happy. Somehow being content equated to being complacent (a fancy way of saying fat and lazy).

As I've gotten older and hopefully wiser that belief has changed. I like being content. And that feels great. It doesn't mean I'm lazy one bit. It means I appreciate what I have and I don't feel a need or worse emptiness longing for more.

I still have many goals. There are still tons of places I want to visit. Things I'd like to own. But, it's OK I don't have that stuff. Those things don't control me. I don't pine for them. I have everything I need.

I am content.
