Handy

One of the things I miss most about not having a house is fixing things. I especially miss my garage and tools. I really used to like restoring things, repairing things, and learning how to tackle new challenges.

To this day I still love going to hardware stores. Even the smell. I suppose most guys are that way.

Yesterday I fixed my office chair as well as fixed the shower handle, and a clogged sink. Super simple stuff. Still it felt good. Some things weren't right. I fixed them. Now they are done. That feeling never gets old.
