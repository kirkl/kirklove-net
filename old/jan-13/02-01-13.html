Simple Wins. Every Time.

Lake Superior by Hiroshi Sugimoto

Out of all the artwork on Art.sy users saved this piece most to their collections. Think about that. Millions of images and this one out performed them all. Seems unlikely this would even standout. Though really it shouldn't be surprising it was chosen ahead of so many others. Why? People crave simplicity and balance and see those characteristics reflected in this piece. Therefore, they are drawn to it. Add to that its abstract nature allows one to overlay their own feelings making it even more appealing to a great number of people.

It's an important reminder that simplicity always trumps other characteristics and a valuable lesson to remember when creating something.




Footnote: I personally love this piece of artwork as well and it reminds me a lot of one of my favorite black and white photos by Shomei Tomatsu.
