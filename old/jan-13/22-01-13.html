No

The Bean's favorite word in the whole wide world is NO. She loves it. She probably says it about 1,000 times a day.

Querés tu leche? - “NO”
Estás consanda? – “NO”
Tenés hambre? - “NO”
Ven aquí. – “NO”
Dame un abrazo. – “NO”

She's a no machine. Even when she doesn't mean it. I could learn a lot from this 15-month old. I've always been bad at at saying no. If someone needs something I instinctively say yes. Even when I don't want to. I don't even know why. Worse, I expect others to say yes when I ask, or come through like I come through for them. They don't. And it stings. But, it's my own fault. I should learn to say no. Maybe I'll just have Bean do all my answering for me.

(FYI: Bean only really understands Spanish right now, hence the examples)
