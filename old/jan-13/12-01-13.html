Saturday's Alright By Me

I love Saturdays. Then again who doesn't. Sleep in a bit (emphasis on a bit), walk with Reese and Bean in Central Park. Pick up an everything bagel for me and a bialy for Paraguay. Take our time eating breakfast as a family. Lounge around and do nothing. Take a nap. Have lunch. Chill. Repeat.

Like I said. I love my Saturdays.
