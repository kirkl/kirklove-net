Still Pimping Canines

I still don't get why no one has heard of this album. It's amazing and yet has only 61 plays on Rdio. That's insane, especially since I account for about 50 plays alone. It really makes me scratch my head why some acts get huge and others wallow in obscurity. This is a great record. If you like well written songs with great lyrics then definitely check this album out.

You can listen here on Bandcamp or here on Rdio

I hope you do. It really is great.
