Peek Inside My Drawers

I'm obsessive compulsive about order. I'll admit that. There could be worse things to be anal about. I just love when things are "in their place". For the longest time I wanted to organize our kitchen drawers. Yeah, even though you don't see anything when they are closed â€“ I knew. I knew there was a hot mess brewing in each one. It bothered the hell out of me.

More importantly, I could never find the stuff I needed when I needed it. It made cooking a pain and cleaning up and putting away stuff even more unenjoyable than it already is. So, I decided to finally tackle it.

I researched the Hafele Wooden Peg System. It's superb for sure, but super costly. It's also a lot of "wood" and I wanted a different texture and something more funky. Somehow I stumbled onto KMN Home completely by accident. And I am beyond delighted I did. Not only is it 1/10th the price of Hafele, it's way more flexible, drop dead simple to install, and comes in a variety of colors. We went with green since that's Paraguay's favorite color.

Man, is it awesome! I used to cringe when I open the drawers and stuff slid everywhere and I could never find anything. Now, I smile. I literally smile when my eyes see that burst of green and perfectly organized drawer (remember I'm anal about this stuff).

If you want a simple, elegant, versatile, and stunning solution to organize drawers (not just kitchen obviously) definitely check out KMN Home. We did a dozen drawers for under $300!
