Happy Birthday Mom

I have the greatest mom in the world. No really, the greatest. And today is her birthday. Happy Birthday Mom.

Hey Internet - say Happy Birthday to my mom!

There really is no way to describe how amazing a woman she is, other than I wouldn't change a thing about her. She's kind, loving, giving, supportive, tough as nails, and the glue that holds our amazing family together. She is a truly special woman and they just don't make 'em like her anymore. I'm beyond grateful for her and everything she has taught me.

I've said it before and I'll said it again - I won the mom lottery.
