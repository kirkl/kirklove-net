I'll Start On Monday

I've always been a "I'll Start On Monday" kind of person. I'm definitely not alone either. Not sure why a lot of us say "Monday" is the day I'll start going back to the gym. Start eating better. Tackle that project I've been putting off, etc. Yet, we do it.

Maybe we want the weekends to ourselves. And with that freedom comes a bit of irresponsibility. I'm really not sure. I've been this way as far as I can remember. I wish I wasn't, but I am.
