Shutting Out The Noise

For a long time I was obsessed with Tech news. Instead of doing something myself the bulk of my time was consumed reading about other folks doing stuff instead. In retrospect that was insanely stupid. Once I figured that out I deleted a bunch of bookmarks and trashed a ton of subscription feeds. It felt really liberating.

Still, I held onto a few. I needed a crutch.

Well now I'm shutting out those as well. Mostly because they are distractions, but also because I find myself getting jealous of other people's progress. Ironically (as if I didn't learn my lesson) reading about other people's progress was greatly impeding mine.

So, if you see me and I'm not up to speed, I'm sorry, I'm heads down and enjoying the silence as well as the focus that comes with that.
