  2. Let go more
  3. Launch, cultivate, and grow PeggSite
  4. Appreciate my family and friends even more
  5. Visit with more people I care about face to face
  6. Explore Brooklyn more with Lili
  7. Hang with The Bean as much as possible
  8. Learn, learn, and learn more
  9. Meditate
