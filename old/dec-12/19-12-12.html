Design On A Dime

It's remarkable how little it costs to have access to extremely robust and highly professional design tools these days. Here's just some of my set up:

  • The incredible Sublime Text 2: $59
  • Adobe Creative Cloud: Adobe's ENTIRE line of products!
    Just $29 a month (with an existing CS3 or higher license)
  • Typekit: Free or just $25 a year for a Personal License
  • Fatcow: web hosting: $49 a year
  • Amazon: Used manuals for dusting off app cobwebs $12
  • Music Subscriptions: (a must!) $20 a month total
  • AWS: Amazon Web Services $1
  • Misc memberships (dribble, treehouse, etc): $100

That's less than $850 TOTAL for an entire year. You could probably do the entire thing for less than $200 and still be totally fine actually (I just like certain tools). Shit, 10 years ago Quark for the Mac would cost you $1,500 alone! Incredible.

Oh and as for my machine, I run a 6-year old Mac Pro. It's a beast of a machine that still kicks ass. We live in amazing times for creators. Get after it. It's cheap as hell to that's for sure.
