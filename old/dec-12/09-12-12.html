Mother & Child

My parents came up for a quick visit. I always love when then do. It's great to see them and even greater to see them with The Bean. Needless to say The Bean is a big hit with the grandparents.

There is something magical about seeing your parents so happy and so in love with your child. It's special and I'm grateful to be able to witness it.
