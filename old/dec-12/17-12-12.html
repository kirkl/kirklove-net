Sometimes It's Just
Never Enough

With some people in this world, it's just never enough. No matter how hard you try to be nice to them, help them, do for them, sacrifice for them, bend for them, change for them, give them all they want, provide constructive feedback, they still aren't fulfilled and somehow you're to blame. It's frustrating on so many levels.

In the end if they want to continue being that way, well, it's on them. At least realizing that, you can go on your way and be happy instead of being sucked down with them.
