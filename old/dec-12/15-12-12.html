Listening To: Canines

Stumbled onto this band (really one guy Joel P West) while checking out a flyer from the Lincoln Center Film Society. It's the soundtrack to a film called "I Am Not A Hipster" which just happens to be playing this upcoming Monday night a block from house! (hell yes, I'm going)

Here is the album embed from Bandcamp. Do listen:



I really like this album a lot. It has a very strong Pedro The Lion Control vibe to it (which happens to be in my top 5 albums of all-time). Looking at Canine's FB page it only has 271 Likes and that's a travesty because this album is phenomenal and more people need to hear it. There is stellar songwriting going on here. Joel P West has a bunch of solo albums, too. I'll definitely be checking them out as well. I love finding hidden gems like this guy.

Joel if you see this, thanks for all the amazing songs. I'm a big fan.
