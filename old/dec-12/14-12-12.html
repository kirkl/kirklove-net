Jesus, I Love
Touching Product

At my core I'm a designer. It's what brings me the most joy in life. Specifically, I love touching and shaping product as well as its branding and voice. I know now I have to do that or I won't be happy. There is no greater feeling in the world than creating something that did not previously exist. One second it's a thought in your head, the next it's a living, breathing thing (albeit mostly digital these days). That's an incredible feeling and I wake up each morning now excited, challenged, and completely energized.
