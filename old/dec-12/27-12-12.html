Numb

Scroll, scroll, scroll. All a blur. Repeat.

That's what online social experiences feel like to me and I am becoming numb to most, if not all the posts. And yet I still do it over and over again. Why? I suppose I've been programmed like a lab rat to do just that. Well, I want to break this ridiculous habit.

It was easy to cut ties with Facebook. That became an obvious waste of time for me and I just stopped visiting altogether. The same thing is happening to a few other sites I used to really love (I never came close to loving Facebook). I just don't "feel" it for these other sites anymore. And yet I still, scroll, scroll, scroll. Repeat.

It's not healthy and more importantly it clouds my mind.
