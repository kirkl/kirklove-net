My Little Corner
Of The World

This is my little home "office".

It's modest though meticulously organized (I'm more than a little anal). I don't need much and this set up suites me just fine. You can't see in the photo, but just to the right is the window that looks out to Central Park. I like taking a break every now and then and just staring out it.

I am a big fan of working from home. I think the old paradigm of sitting in a office, getting less work done actually, is dying. Plus, I can't beat the commute.

I'm really happy in my little corner of the world. Find yours.
