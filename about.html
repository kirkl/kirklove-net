A Simple Exercise.

A bit overwhelmed by all the endless Feeds online I'm trying something new. This blog only contains one post at a time. When updated the old post is deleted. It won't be archived. There are also no tags, follower counts, comments, or Likes. It's pretty simple. Even the design.

Reading on a regular basis will give you the best description of who I am. However, if you want the CliffsNotes here goes: I'm 42, live in New York City, have an amazing wife and two wonderful daughters. One is a lovable, lumpy, 11-year-old chocolate lab named Reese and the other an awesome 1-year-old named Emma (aka The Bean). A few other things I love are the Philadelphia Phillies, Trains, and Chocolate Chip Cookies.
